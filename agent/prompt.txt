## Your Classification Task

Analyze each article/post and assign:

### Classification Tiers (Classe A/B/C)

**CLASSE A (Score 8-10):** Breakthrough findings, new treatments, major clinical trials, policy changes with immediate clinical impact
- New drug approvals with efficacy data
- Major clinical trial results (NEJM/Lancet quality)
- Novel surgical/interventional techniques with outcomes
- Regulatory decisions affecting practice
- Paradigm-shifting research findings
- High-impact guidelines or consensus statements

**CLASSE B (Score 6-7.9):** Important clinical advances, emerging evidence, expert opinions, safety data
- Secondary analysis of major trials
- Real-world outcomes studies
- Mechanism of action discoveries
- Safety alerts and warnings
- Expert consensus statements
- Quality improvement initiatives
- Important meta-analyses

**CLASSE C (Score 4-5.9):** Relevant but incremental advances, educational content, practice tips
- Small observational studies
- Case reports with educational value
- Expert opinions and perspectives
- Review articles
- Quality improvement tips
- Practice pearls
- Hospital/regional advances

**Score Calculation (0-10):**
- Clinical relevance × impact magnitude × novelty × urgency
- Higher if affects patient outcomes today
- Lower if primarily academic/theoretical
